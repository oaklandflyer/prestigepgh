About

Pittsburgh Prestige Remodeling was founded in 2020 by Michael Allen, a lifelong craftsman with a passion for building things that last. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted name across the Pittsburgh area — known for precision, reliability, and work that speaks for itself.

From hand-built kitchen cabinets to custom built-ins and finish trim, every project is approached with the same standard: Elegance, Hand Crafted. We're licensed, insured, and Pittsburgh-proud.
